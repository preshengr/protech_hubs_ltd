How quickly can you respond to an enquiry?

+

We guarantee a response to all enquiries within 4 business hours. For urgent IT incidents on our managed service plans, our NOC is available 24/7.

Do you offer a free initial consultation?

+

Yes — absolutely. We offer a complimentary 30-minute strategy call with one of our senior consultants. No commitment required. This helps us understand your environment before making any recommendations.

What industries do you serve?

+

We serve clients across financial services, telecommunications, healthcare, education, oil & gas, government agencies, and retail. Our solutions are adapted to the regulatory and operational realities of each sector.

Do you work with businesses outside Abuja?

+

Yes. While our headquarters is in Abuja, we deliver projects nationwide — Lagos, Port Harcourt, Kano, Enugu, and beyond. Most services are delivered remotely, with on-site visits for installations and assessments.

What does a Managed IT Support contract involve?

+

Our managed services contracts cover 24/7 infrastructure monitoring, helpdesk support, patch management, backup oversight, and regular reporting. Contracts are monthly with flexible SLA tiers based on your business requirements.
